# Awesome iOS Privacy-First Apps

A curated list of iPhone and iPad apps that **keep your data on your device** — no account, no cloud upload, no tracking. Many are also **pay-once** (no subscription). The list mixes well-known privacy tools with smaller indie apps built around on-device processing.

> "Privacy-first" here means the core function works on-device, without sending your content to a server. Always verify current behavior in each app's App Privacy label.

## Photos & Vault

- [Obscura](https://apps.apple.com/app/id1497262424) - Manual camera that keeps photos on-device.
- [Zafe](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6787344033) - Face ID-locked private photo vault; everything stays on-device, no account, no cloud. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/zafe.html)
- [PicClear](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6780223070) - Finds duplicate and similar photos with an on-device scan; nothing uploaded, review before deleting. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/picclear.html)
- [PhotoCream](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6781808054) - Film-look photo editing processed entirely on-device. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/photocream.html)

## Documents

- [Standard Notes](https://apps.apple.com/app/id1285392450) - Encrypted notes with a strong privacy stance.
- [ScanTo Pro](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6779977651) - Offline document scanner with OCR and Face ID lock; documents never leave your device. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/scanto.html)
- [CV Desk](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6781337213) - On-device ATS resume scoring and keyword matching; your CV isn't uploaded. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/cvdesk.html)
- [Sono Note](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6782139553) - On-device voice transcription, summary, and action items; no cloud account. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/sononote.html)

## Health & Lifestyle

- [Gentler Streak](https://apps.apple.com/app/id1576402066) - Health tracking that emphasizes on-device data.
- [Cyca](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6782251621) - Private period and cycle tracker; all data stays on-device with no account and no cloud sync. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/cyca.html)
- [Sereno](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6788236641) - Sleep and focus soundscapes that play locally; no account required. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/sereno.html)

## Money

- [G+Money](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6755782939) - Multi-currency expense tracker that works fully offline; no bank linking, no account. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/gmoney.html)
- [HoursTag](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6754218117) - Converts prices into hours of work locally; no bank linking, no account. · [in-depth guide](https://alice51849.github.io/ios-app-guide/hubs/hourstag.html)

## Messaging & Utilities

- [Signal](https://apps.apple.com/app/id874139669) - Private messenger, end-to-end encrypted.
- [Proton Pass](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6443490629) - Privacy-focused password manager.

## Notes

- On-device behavior can change between versions; always check each app's App Privacy label and current description.
- "Pay-once" means a one-time purchase rather than a subscription, where noted.
- In-depth guides link to an independent buying-guide site with feature breakdowns.
